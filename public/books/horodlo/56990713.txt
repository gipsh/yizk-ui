The expelled in O Khan The appearance of the Horodlar Jews has changed a lot in the years since they were expelled from Horodla. The twenty men were forced to shave off their beards, which greatly changed their appearance, except Shattirv, who kept his beard until his last day. .


Nitel fails in her children.


Our poor little girl became less and less, our whole food supply dwindled for four weeks. The bread was moldy. This was the bread that was still baked in Harodla. And people ate it in small portions for the sake of frugality and prudence. Despite this lack, my mother did not forget to add some of her bread, from time to time, to hungry people who had nothing to eat, adding that in this merit, her children should be saved from starvation. .

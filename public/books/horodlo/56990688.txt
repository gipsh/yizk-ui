My brother Fishel and I decided not to stay even one day with the Germans, and before Hosanna Rabbah we crossed the river to Ostile and from there we left for Ludmir, during my time.


Haim Drucker.


Father Zel and my mother refused to leave their possessions and stayed together with my sisters in Horodla. When we arrived in Ludmir, we went to our uncle who welcomed us with great joy. My uncle insisted that we should write to my parents and have them rush over to us in Ludmir. I returned to Ostile and sent a letter to mine.

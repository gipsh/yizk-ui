The aunt, Bird Fige, with her husband Shmuel and their five children, sons and daughters, were all killed. .


The aunt Hema, my husband Fishel and their three children, who lived in Helm, were all killed. .


Our aunt Esther was the youngest of the big and beautiful family, she lived in Ostile, with her husband and two children, none of them remained. .


Aunt Mary, uncle Moshe's wife, and her children were saved, they live today in Israel and America. It was a family of scholars, people of prayer, with one word: Torah and goods. .

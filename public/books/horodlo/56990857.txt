In memory of the Horodla community (Horubishov district - Poland) God will remember the Horodla community.


and Judea, and we will avenge the blood of our dear brothers and sisters who were destroyed together with their Rabbi - their leader in the people of Tefarat Benim - and Moshe's law against the Germans and their followers Yamesh, in the holocaust of the Jews of Europe in the Second World War. Their precious and pure memory will not be fulfilled by us forever those who commemorate: the descendants of Horodla in Israel and in the Diaspora.
